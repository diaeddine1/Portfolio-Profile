Reading Light Novels And Manhuas

I enjoy reading light novels, such as The Beginning After the End and Lord of the Mysteries, as well as exploring manhuas, which combine engaging storytelling with striking artwork. Light novels, a popular form of Japanese literature, are known for their fast-paced narratives, immersive world-building, and anime-style illustrations, often appealing to young adult readers. These hobbies have cultivated my appreciation for creative storytelling, diverse cultural perspectives, and visual artistry, inspiring my imagination and enhancing my analytical thinking.
